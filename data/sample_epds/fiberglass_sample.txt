PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

GlassBatt R-20 is a residential-grade mineral fiber batt insulation manufactured
from a combination of recycled glass cullet (minimum 53% post-consumer recycled
content) and virgin silica sand, bonded with a formaldehyde-free acrylic binder.
The product is designed for friction-fit installation in 2x6 wood-frame wall
cavities at nominal 5.5-inch thickness.

DECLARED UNIT

1 square metre of installed fiberglass batt insulation at a nominal thermal
resistance of RSI 3.52 (R-20 imperial) and an installed thickness of 140 mm.

SYSTEM BOUNDARIES: A1–A3 (Cradle to Gate)

Modules A1 through A3 are included. Modules A4 through D are excluded from
this declaration and are not included in totals.

Life Cycle Inventory data sourced from the DATASMART US/CA database v4.2 and
Owens Corning primary production data (2021 calendar year average).
