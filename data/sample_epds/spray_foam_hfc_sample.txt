PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

ThermoSeal HFC-245fa is a two-component, closed-cell spray polyurethane foam (ccSPF)
insulation system intended for residential and commercial wall, roof, and floor
assemblies. The product is installed on-site by trained applicators.

The blowing agent used is HFC-245fa (1,1,1,3,3-pentafluoropropane), a
hydrofluorocarbon with a global warming potential of approximately 858 CO2e
(100-year GWP, AR5).

IMPORTANT NOTE ON BIOGENIC CARBON:
This product contains no biogenic carbon content. The raw materials are
entirely petroleum-derived. The biogenic carbon content is 0 kg CO2e per
functional unit. Do not confuse this value with the total GWP figure below.

DECLARED UNIT

1 square metre of installed insulation at a nominal thermal resistance of
RSI 3.52 (equivalent to R-20 imperial) and a typical installed thickness of
approximately 89 mm (3.5 inches).
